Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

At Cloud Closet ("we," "us," or "our"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our wardrobe management and laundry services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services effectively:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Delivery Information: Home address, access codes, or special delivery instructions.
  • Service Data: Preferences regarding washing scents, ironing styles, and wardrobe inventory.
  • Payment Data: Processed securely through our third-party payment providers (we do not store full credit card numbers).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our pickup and delivery services.
  • To manage your digital wardrobe inventory in the app.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders, updates, or emergency "Flash Deliveries."
  • To improve our services based on feedback and usage patterns.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is stored on secure servers located within Switzerland and the European Union. We retain your data only as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third parties only when necessary for the service, such as:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  • Logistics partners for delivery (if not handled directly by our fleet).
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and Swiss nFADP), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data ("Right to be forgotten").
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure functionality and performance cookies to analyze site traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
